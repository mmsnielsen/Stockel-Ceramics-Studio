Stockel Ceramics is a welcoming creative community space. Dedicated in supporting our members and workshop students in their ceramics journey, whether they are a beginner or a seasoned pro.

Gallery Studio Shop

Our Studio space

Located in Kraainen, Stockel Ceramics is a short walk from Stockel Square (Place Dumon), where you'll find a number of shops and cafes. Here we have created a calm and welcoming environment where you can explore your pottery journey at your own pace. With a focus on craftsmanship, community engagement, and artistic expression. We aim to be a space for all to come and slow down, be present, connect with your hands and creativity, regardless of skill level.

Weekly Classes

From beginner workshops to advanced techniques, we offer classes for all skill levels.

Learn the fundamentals of working on the pottery wheel in a supportive setting. Perfect for beginners and intermediate potters alike. The course also covers glazing and firings* so you will have a chance to complete pieces by the end of the course.

Memberships

Our memberships are designed to provide flexible access to our studio space and resources, fostering a supportive community for ceramic artists of all levels.

Mini Studio Membership

€200 / Month

Perfect for casual potters.

  • 20 hours of studio access per month
  • Access to shared tools, equipment and (under)glazes
  • Clay available for purchase at a discount
  • Completed firings €10 per kilo
  • Your own storage shelf
  • Access to sell in our studio shop*
Most Popular

Full Studio Membership

€350 / Month

Ideal for dedicated ceramic artists.

  • 40 hours of studio access per month
  • Access to shared tools, equipment and (under)glazes
  • Custom kiln firing requests**
  • Clay available for purchase at a discount
  • Completed firings €10 per kilo
  • Your own storage shelf
  • Access to sell in our studio shop*

Student Drop-in Access

€20 / Session

For students attending weekly classes or who have completed a class, who want additional independent time to work on their pieces.

  • 3 hours per drop-in session during studio opening hours
  • Same benefits as in classes except tuition
  • Additional clay available for purchase at a discount

All members should have prior pottery studio experience rather than production-scale pottery work.
Contact us to discuss which membership option is best for you and/or a studio tour.
*Studio shop access is subject to approval by the studio manager.
**Custom kiln firing requests are subject to approval and scheduling by the studio manager.
