About Our Studio
Located in Kraainem, we are a short walk to Stockel Square (Place Dumon) with plenty of transport connections, cafes and shops. With a focus on craftsmanship, community engagement, and artistic expression. We aim to be a space for all to come and slow down, be present, connect with your hands and creativity, regardless of skill level.
